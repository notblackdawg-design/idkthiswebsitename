## 2\. What Are Cookies and Local Storage?

**Cookies** are small text files stored in your browser by a website you visit. They allow the website to remember information about your visit.

**Local storage** is a similar technology built into your browser that allows websites to store data locally on your device. Unlike cookies, local storage data is not automatically sent to the server with every request — it stays on your device until you clear it.

Buildboard uses both cookies and local storage, explained in detail below.

\---

## 3\. What Buildboard Stores in Your Browser

### 3.1 Session Token (Cookie)

* **What it is:** A secure authentication token generated by Supabase when you log into your account
* **Why we use it:** To keep you logged in between page visits without requiring you to enter your password every time
* **Duration:** 7 days from your last login, or until you log out
* **Who sets it:** Supabase (our authentication and database provider)
* **Can you disable it:** If you disable this cookie you will not be able to stay logged in. You can still browse Buildboard anonymously without it.

### 3.2 Theme Preference (Local Storage)

* **What it is:** A simple value storing your chosen display theme — either "light", "dark", or "system"
* **Why we use it:** To remember your display preference so you do not have to reset it every visit
* **Duration:** Until you clear your browser's local storage or change the setting yourself
* **Who sets it:** Buildboard directly
* **Can you disable it:** Yes. If cleared, the platform will default to light mode until you set a preference again.

### 3.3 Guest ID (Local Storage)

* **What it is:** A randomly generated unique identifier (UUID) created the first time an anonymous user submits a post
* **Why we use it:** To allow anonymous users to delete their own posts and to enforce rate limits without requiring an account. This ID is never shared with other users or displayed anywhere on the platform.
* **Duration:** Until you clear your browser's local storage
* **Who sets it:** Buildboard directly
* **Can you disable it:** If cleared, you will lose the ability to delete your previously submitted anonymous posts. A new Guest ID will be generated the next time you post anonymously.

## 6\. How to Control and Clear Cookies

You can control, clear, or block cookies and local storage through your browser settings. Here is how to do it in common browsers:

**Google Chrome:** Settings → Privacy and Security → Clear Browsing Data → select Cookies and Site Data and Local Storage

**Mozilla Firefox:** Settings → Privacy \& Security → Cookies and Site Data → Clear Data

**Safari:** Settings → Safari → Advanced → Website Data → Remove All Website Data

**Microsoft Edge:** Settings → Privacy, Search, and Services → Clear Browsing Data → Cookies and Other Site Data

**On mobile (Chrome):** Settings → Privacy → Clear Browsing Data

Please note that clearing cookies and local storage will:

* Log you out of your Buildboard account
* Reset your theme preference to the default
* Remove your Guest ID, meaning you will no longer be able to delete your anonymous posts
